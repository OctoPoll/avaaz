Zariya

Connect to Justice

Zariya makes justice actionable and accessible to women facing violence.
By providing a risk-free method, Zariya paves women’s paths from being victims
to being survivors with a right and will to justice.

Report anonymously and safely an incident of violence against women. Take the first step to exercise your rights

Get a quick first-instance connection to a service provider who can guide you. You can connect with a legal expert, women’s bank representative, psychologist, medical practitioner or Indian Police Service’s women’s units.

Zariya facilitates the transparency and accountability needed to challenge social stigma and corruption. We aim to expedite women’s justice processes. Here, you can track whether or not your needs are met. You will have to create an account for your record and case details to be maintained.
