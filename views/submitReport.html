Zariya makes justice actionable and accessible to women facing gender-based violence.
By providing a risk-free way for women to voice their experiences,
Zariya paves women’s paths from feeling like victims to being survivors with a right and will to justice.

In India, for each reported rape, 30 go unreported. Women avoid reporting out of fear of dishonor or lack of access to police stations and courts. They may not want to show their face and disclose their identity to unsympathetic listeners. For Zariya, protecting a woman’s identity is paramount. Anonymity is maintained until and unless a woman chooses to speak with a care provider. Even at that stage, the woman’s identity will only be known internally and managed confidentially.
